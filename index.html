Cerebral Oximeter

Single-Channel NIRS Relative Oxygenation Monitor — Rev 3.2

EE 201 - Biomedical Instrumentation POTS Demonstration System Prototype 1

Overview

A breadboard-level near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS) device that detects relative changes in cerebral oxygenation through the human forehead. Two LEDs (730 nm and 850 nm) illuminate tissue while a BPW34 photodiode measures returned light, enabling visualization of oxygenation trends during postural changes associated with POTS.

Signal chain: photodiode → MCP6022 transimpedance amplifier (100 kΩ) → second-stage gain (48 V/V) → ADS1115 ADC (64 SPS) → Arduino Nano 33 IoT with 4-state TDM and EMA filtering.
