About Vishay Siliconix

Vishay Siliconix is a subsidiary of Vishay Intertechnology, a global manufacturer of passive electronic components.
Vishay Siliconix specializes in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of power MOSFETs, IGBTs, and other power semiconductors.
The company was founded in 1962 and is headquartered in Santa Clara, California.
Vishay Siliconix provides power solutions for a variety of applications, including renewable energy systems, industrial equipment, computing and telecommunications, and automotive electronics.
The company's products are designed for high efficiency, low power consumption, and high reliability, and are manufactured in state-of-the-art facilities using advanced fabrication processes.
With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, Vishay Siliconix is committed to providing its customers with the best power management solutions to meet their needs.
