Usage Rule for Optics Verification
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) Prefer S1/S3 manufacturer datasheets for numeric decisions.
2) Use S2/S4 for product-page context and links.
3) Use S5/S6 only as supplemental evidence where exact-MPN primary coverage is incomplete.
4) Record any claim with missing primary citation as unverified until resolved.
